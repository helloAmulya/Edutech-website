Frequently Asked Questions

1. What services does Shyampari Edutech offer?

We provide personalized home tutoring services across various subjects, focusing on helping students excel in their academic journey. Our services include one-on-one tutoring, expert-led courses, and counseling sessions to guide students through their learning.

2. How do I sign up for tutoring services?

Simply visit our *Get Started* page and fill out the form with your details. Once we receive your request, our team will contact you to match you with a suitable tutor based on your subject preferences and learning goals.

3. Are the tutoring sessions online or in-person?

We offer both in-person and online tutoring sessions. You can choose the option that works best for you when filling out the sign-up form.

4. How can I book a session with a tutor?

After registering, you'll be able to browse available tutors and their schedules, then select the time slot that works for you. Booking can be done directly from your profile page.

5. What subjects are covered in tutoring sessions?

We offer tutoring in a wide range of subjects including Mathematics, Science, English, Social Studies, and more. We also provide specialized courses and exam preparation assistance.

6. What if I am not satisfied with my tutor?

If you're not satisfied with your tutor, let us know. We'll work to find a better match for your needs or issue a refund, depending on the situation.
